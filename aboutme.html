About Me

With a passion for blending digital creativity and cutting-edge hardware solutions, I strive to shape tomorrow's technology today through intuitive design and seamless integration.

Bridging Passion for Technology with a Legacy of Innovation

My name is Crystal Wernicke, a dedicated tech enthusiast and entrepreneur based in SWFL. With a foundation in Software Application Development from Rasmussen University and an Associates in Arts from Florida Southwestern State College, where I led the Computer Science and Technology Club as President, I've embarked on a dynamic journey.

As Co-Organizer of SWFL Coders and Tech Alliance of SWFL, and founder of ProtoAlchemy Labs LLC in May 2023, I specialize in crafting custom web applications and rapid prototyping solutions.

Transitioning into 3D Printing Engineering in June 2023, I furthered my impact as Organizer of SWFL Hackerspace, hosting workshops on cutting-edge topics like 3D Printing and E-Waste Repurposing.

With a lifelong passion sparked by my father's influence as a computer engineer, I blend hands-on experience with a commitment to continual learning and teaching, catalyzed by over a decade managing kitchens before my tech evolution in 2019.
